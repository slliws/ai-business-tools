6. Tableau

What it is: Tableau is a powerful data visualization tool.

How it helps: Tableau uses AI to help you analyze and visualize your data in real-time. For example, the "Ask Data" feature allows users to type natural language questions about their data, and Tableau will respond with relevant visualizations.

What it is: Google Analytics is a web analytics service that tracks and reports website traffic.

How it helps: With AI-driven insights, Google Analytics can help businesses understand user behavior more deeply. For example, it can predict which customers are likely to convert based on their browsing history, enabling targeted marketing strategies.
